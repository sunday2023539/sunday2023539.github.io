Research focused on thematic mapping techniques, geovisualization, cartographic communication, and population distribution visualization.

Assessment of Thematic Mapping Techniques for Population Distribution Visualization with Simulation Support: A Comparative Study of Dot Density, Filled Isopleth, and Unfilled Isopleth Maps

This research investigates the effectiveness of thematic mapping techniques for visualizing population distribution patterns using simulation-supported spatial analysis. The study compares Dot Density Maps, Filled Isopleths, and Unfilled Isopleths to evaluate how different cartographic representations influence spatial interpretation, visual perception, and user understanding.

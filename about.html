About

My Background

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Physics and I am currently completing my MSc in Applied Geoinformatics at the University of Salzburg. My academic journey has allowed me to combine analytical problem-solving from physics with geospatial technologies, spatial analysis, and data-driven decision-making.Throughout my studies, I have gained experience in GIS, cartography, geovisualization, advanced remote sensing, spatial databases, WebGIS, geospatial data acquisition, and spatial data analysis. I have also worked with programming languages and analytical tools used for geospatial processing, mapping, visualization, and data management. I work as a Data Analyst where I develop SQL-based reporting workflows, analyze datasets, and create data-driven insights to support decision-making. This combination of geospatial and analytical expertise has strengthened my interest in integrating GIS, spatial analysis, data analytics, and business intelligence techniques to solve real-world problems.
